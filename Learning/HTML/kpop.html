KPop Demon Hunters: Music Meets Fantasy

KPop Demon Hunters is a groundbreaking animated musical film that blends the dazzling world of K-pop with thrilling demon-slaying adventures. It celebrates identity, empowerment, and culture while captivating fans across the globe.

The Story

Follow Huntr/x — a K-pop girl group leading double lives as performers and demon hunters. Their story explores friendship, acceptance, and courage as they battle the Saja Boys, a rival group hiding a dark secret.

The Music

The soundtrack became a global sensation with hits like “Golden” and “Takedown”. Blending catchy K-pop beats with cinematic energy, the music topped Billboard charts and became anthems for fans worldwide.

Global Impact

The film quickly became Netflix’s most-watched animated release of all time. With its mix of Korean culture, mythology, and artistry, KPop Demon Hunters sparked a worldwide movement that is already expanding into sequels and more.
