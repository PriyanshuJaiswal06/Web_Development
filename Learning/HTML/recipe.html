Besan Ladoo (Gram Flour Sweet Balls)

Ingredients (for ~15 ladoos)

  • 2 cups besan (gram flour)
  • ¾ cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • Water (ofcourse)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar (or adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp chopped nuts (optional – almonds, cashews, pistachios)s

Instructions to make this delightfull Sweet

  1. Roast the besan
    • Heat a heavy-bottomed pan or kadhai
    • Add ghee and let it melt on low flame.
    • Roast until it turns golden brown, gives a nutty aroma, and ghee starts releasing (about 15–20 minutes).
  2. Cool a Bit
    • Remove the pan from heat.
    • Let the mixture cool slightly (warm, not hot – otherwise sugar will melt and make ladoos sticky).
  3. Add Sugar & Flavor
    • Mix in powdered sugar and cardamom powder.
    • Stir well so the sugar blends evenly.
    • Add chopped nuts or raisins if you like.
  4. Shape the Ladoos
    • Take small portions of the mixture in your palms.
    • Roll them into round ladoos while still warm.
  5. Set & Store
    • Let the ladoos cool completely.
    • Store in an airtight container at room temperature (they last 2 weeks easily).

Tips for Perfect Ladoos:

  • Roast on low flame only – patience is key.
  • If mixture feels dry while binding, add 1–2 tsp melted ghee.
  • For extra richness, you can add a pinch of saffron soaked in warm milk.
